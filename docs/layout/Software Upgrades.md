# Updating your software
{: .no_toc }

The DiscoX software is always being improved. To keep your device working well and get the latest features, it’s a good idea to update it to the newest official version whenever you can. In this chapter, you’ll learn how to easily update your device so it keeps running smoothly.

## Connecting the DiscoX to your PC

The device has a latching button switch to control power to the device, press the button for at least 50ms to turn the device on and press it again to turn the device off.
