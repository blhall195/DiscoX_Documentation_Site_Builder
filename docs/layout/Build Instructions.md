# How to make your own DiscoX
{: .no_toc }

The DiscoX is priced such that considering individual component postage and possible build mistakes it's almost certainly cheaper to buy a DiscoX pre-built than to make your own. That said one of the core design features of the DiscoX is that it is relativley modular. Meaning as time goes on and they inevitibly break, they should be relativley cheap to repair.
